Abstract

Esther's predilection for organic forms, nature and different natural and artificial materials has been continuously infused into her recent work. Light wave is a light installation creates a psychedelic vibration through its natural and organic visual expression. The varying colors and nature sounds are sampled and collected by Esther in different NYC parks.
